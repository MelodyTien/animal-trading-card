Pomeranian

The Pomeranian is a descendant of the sled dogs of Iceland and Laplan. It is the smallest member of the spitz family of dogs.

  • Weight: 3 to 7 pounds (1.3 to 3.2 kg)
  • Height: 6 to 7 inches (15 to 18 cm)
  • Life Expectancy: 12 to 16 years

Pomeranian gets this name for the reason that it was developed at Pomerania, the areas of Germany and Poland. It has long, double coat in many colors. The most common colors are red, orange, cream, sable, black, brown, and blue.
